Cast

✻

Introducing our cast featuring students from 7th to 12th grade, old boys and teachers!

Joshua Tachil

'Billy'

Joshua is a playful prankster at heart, and acting is his blood. Bright and keen, he loves to observe friends & family and imitate them to their own amusement.In 2016, Joshua played Nellie in the musical ‘Clowns’, directed by Rebecca Jacob at the Greenwood High School in Bangalore.Joshua has a good sense of rhythm, and has been singing since he was a young boy. He has participated in various contests and recently won first place for solo singing in the OBA Buzz held in SJBHS.

Noel Joseph Prabhu

'Billy'

An enthusiastic 12 year-old, he believes that doing what he loves most that keeps him happy. He started performing at the age of 2 ½ years and his first piano recital was at the age of 4 years.He played the lead role as Oliver Twist in the Broadway Musical Production “Oliver with a Twist”, a William Joseph Academy production. He has performed with the Thailand Sanctuary Symphony Orchestra in an Indo-Thai Music Concert in Thailand as a soloist and as part of the Chorale under the baton of Doc.He has competed in many events like, solo voice, piano and drumming. He firmly believes that his victory lies in participation and not in focusing on the end result, and that is what keeps him going.

Pranav Ashwin

'Tony'

17 year old Pranav Ashwin Ramesh plays ‘Tony’ in the Broadway musical. He represented SJBHS in the under 16 basketball team in 2015 and the under 16 & 18 basketball team from 2016-2017. He is the representative of one of the four houses in his school. He was the event head for the designing event of the science and tech fest ‘Transcendence’. His passions lie in photography and filmography, and he aspires to become a designer.

Divin Kamath

'Tony'

Being a ambitious person I aim to fulfill my dream and love of being an actor in the industry along with becoming a successful ACCA in the corporate field. Along with acting Dancing and my personality is something always look to work on. He always look to make every day count and strive hard with my dedication and I see this opportunity as a stepping stone to a phase of my career and hope to see many more in the future

Ashwin Pradeep

'Dad ~ Jackie'

This 17-year old Ashwin's first time being part of a Broadway production. Other than being a part of various school plays, he has mindfully derived most of his acting skills from movies and live plays. He is a good mimic and can, therefore, emulate emotions and methods of speech well. His excitement and enthusiasm is unmatched.

Johan Peter

'Michael'

Johan is a playful and energetic young boy. An incredible actor, and talented dancer, he has placed in various events held at his school. He's a great team player and with his cheerful nature gets along with teammates and friends exceptionally well. He has also represented his school in cultural events like mock rock and air crash. His personality shines both on and off stage and successfully secured the third place in the Personality completion held in OBA Buzz 2018.

Cherish Carvalho

'Mrs. Wilkinson'

Cherish is a quirky, out-going and charismatic 17 year old. After taking part in numerous plays and productions in school, including 'Over the Rainbow' by Kevin Oliver and 'A book of Carols' By Maya Mascherenes and Judith Roby Bidapa,, Cherish discovered her love for acting and theatre. Cherish also represented SJBHS as part of the fashion team at Phenomenon and Esplendida. Cherish has also represented the state and won on multiple occasions in the field of sports.

Darshana Ritesh

'Mrs. Wilkinson'

Darshana is one of the most accomplished and dedicated people on the team. Alongside her close friend, the two confounded one of Josephs' most esteemed events, the musical. A lover of dance and acting, she has been involved in theatre since she was in the eighth grade and has even excelled in the field of public speaking, debates and conferences. Furthermore her diplomacy and analytical thinking has also been displayed profoundly by her placements in 3 out of 4 MUNs and 2 out of 3 commerce fest. Darshana is involved in many sports, especially basketball. A sensational actor, diligent worker and a studious girl.

Christin Sarah

'Debbie'

With her minimal previous experience in musical theatre is looking to use this opportunity as a steppingstone to future openings in the same. Has taken interest in the dance aspect of theatre as dancing is something she’s passionate about. A keen lover of music who has always been part of choirs since her childhood. She is eternally indebted to everyone who made this production a reality!

Mariam Kabir

'Debbie'

Everyone who knows Mariam knows her as an enthusiastic and a fun loving person. Mariam’s loved by most of her peers, has the ability to bond with anyone and loves working in a team. As a child she’s always loved doing plays and has a profound passion for acting and theatre. She has been a part of a number of plays and hence has rightfully earned the title of being a dramaqueen. Having been interested in football, she was a part of her school football team for a very long time & has a keen interest in the sport. mariam is a very convincing & confident person and public speaking is her forte. Trust us when we say that this girl can talk herself out of any situation! Having done both her trinity and lamda exams, she has fair bit of experience and aspires to become an eminent lawyer one day. All these qualities combined with her child like innocence makes her the perfect person to portray Debbie, one of the female characters in this production and our very own billy’s love interest.

Ranjana Monnappa

'Grandma'

Ranjana Monnappa is a bi-lingual actor and she is delighted to be back in front of the floodlights in this production of Billy Elliot, playing the role of the grandmother. She was last seen on stage as the female protagonist, Eliza Doolittle, in My Fair Lady, an adaptation of George Bernard Shaw's Pygmalion, staged at Hymamshu Jyothi Kala Peetha. Back in the 80’s she has acted in a couple of one act Konkani plays in the Canara Union Arts Section. As an experienced higher secondary English teacher, she finds ample scope to use her thespian skills.

Adarshika M

'Grandma'

Adarshika is an exhuberant girl with raw talent in the field of public speaking and theatrical expression, both on and off stage. She has completed 7 years of ballet in Yana Lewis Dance Academy with 4 stage shoes and has been an active participant in fests and other such events. In 2015, she developed a prototype app along with her teammates for WeTech. She is also a gifted team leader adept with many a skill. She has won best secretary award in BGHS and has been part of the Baldwin Democratic Organisation for 5 years. She also played an important role in the organising committee of BDO fest and in the basketball team of her previous school. Quick on her feet and light on her toes, we present to you Adarshika as 'Grandmother'.

Shaun Paul

'Big Davey'

17 year-old Shaun Paul is someone who loves playing a part and enjoys theatrics. The entire world’s a stage for this one, as his carefree and uninhibited personality shows itself at any given opportunity. Having acted in small scale plays at school and in various literary events, he is a self-taught actor. His interests lay in cultural and literary competitions, primarily Jest/Just a Minute’s (JAMs). He makes it his life’s goal to make people laugh and lighten moods no matter how tense a situation.

Yash Menda

'Big Davey'

Yash Menda began acting in kindergarten. Ever since childhood Yash has been fascinated and attracted to the stage and the spotlight. He loves dancing to Bollywood music and aspires to make it big in the Bollywood industry as an actor. He admires Varun Dhawan and Tom Cruise. Yash has both intellectual and physical skills. He has been a consistent proficiency achiever at school and has been into enhancing his physique since 2014. He also boxes and also qualified to the state level recently.

Denzel Joyson

'George'

Denzel Joyson began acting at a very early age when he had to pretend to understand Math in school. He was involved in several school level productions and became heavily involved in the Bangalore underground metal scene in 2016. He plays for two bands Cælum (a progressive metal band) and Raven (a black metal project) as the bassist.Besides music and acting Denzel enjoys debating and public speaking and has won several accolades in the past. He can also be funny at times, sometimes described as a poor man's Chandler Bing.
