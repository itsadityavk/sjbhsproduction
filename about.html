About St. Joseph's Boys' High School

✻

‘Fide et Labore’ – This motto has guided generations of Josephites for well over a century and a half at our celebrated institution, St. Joseph’s Boys’ High School. Translated from Latin, ‘Faith and Toil’ is an integral part of our institution’s culture and is endorsed by every person who has walked through these gates. Our way of life is to strive for excellence by inspiring countless generations to give their best.

This year SJBHS celebrates 160 year of educational excellence. In order to commemorate the same, as well as to facilitate and encourage our students’ diverse talents and provide them with a platform where they can showcase and develop their artistry by conducting a Broadway musical.

About Billy Elliot : The Musical

✻

‘Billy Elliot: The Musical’ is set during the 1984 coal miner’s strike in Durham, England. The musical tells the story of a young boy named Billy, who discovers an unconventional passion for ballet. However, his desire to pursue dance is not looked upon kindly by the mining community in which he has been raised. The musical celebrates the trials and triumphs that accompany Billy in the pursuit of his dreams.
